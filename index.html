Samir Limbu
Product Designer based in London

About me

I was born in Nepal but spent a greater part of my growing days in India where I received my formal high school education. I moved to the UK with my family in 2007. I received my Bachelor's in Computer Graphic Technology from Kingston University. My experience lies in investigating and mapping out the needs and pains of a user to design a simple and seamless solution. I have worked in highly collaborative environments with cross-disciplinary teams of engineers and QA's taking ownership of the project from start to end. I’m currently working as a Product Designer at Preqin to help build a SaaS platform to help General Partners with Fundraising workflow.

Process

  • Discovery work with internal stakeholders and Product team to get better insights into business goals and understand the user's needs.
  • Define how we can help out the user by reviewing quantitative and qualitative research data.
  • Ideation process to generate different ideas to solve the user's problem and add value to the product.
  • Develop ideas into wireframes and test design with the user and collect feedback. Make design iterations based on the user feedback.
